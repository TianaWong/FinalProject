SPONSOR A BASKET

The Chinatown Partnership has placed 48 new, steel, green trash cans around Chinatown. Many local businesses and individuals have joined the effort by sponsoring a litter basket. A plaque on each basket reads "A cleaner Chinatown starts here" in English and "A cleaner Chinatown depends on you and me" in Chinese. To find out more, or to to sponsor a basket, contact us.
