FUTURE EVENTS

5.03 // 36th Asian American & Pacific Islander Heritage Festival Heitage Month

11am-4pm @ Mott Street (btwn Canal & Worth Streets)

For the first-time ever, the APA Heritage Festival will take place in Chinatown. We invite everyone to return home to celebrate our culture, heritage and oneness of spirit. Enjoy stage performances, food from restaurants and eateries, info booths & arts and crafts for kids.



6.18 // 8th Annual Chinatown Partnership Benefit and Award Gala

6pm-10pm @ Resturanaut

Join us and celebrate the contribution Chinatown Partnership have done for the downtown community. Enjoy dinner, stage performances and giveaways. Latest registeration by 6.18 at 12pm.

7.26 // 25th Silver Anniversary of the Americans with Disabilities Act

11am-4pm @ Mott Street (btwn Canal & Worth Street)

Celebrate the silver anniversary of ADA. Enjoy stage performances, food from restaurants and eateries, info booths, face painting & arts and crafts for kids.



8.05 // Mid-Autumn Moon Festival Night Market

3pm-10pm @ Forsyth Street (btwn East Broadway & Division Street)

Celebrate the autumn harvest in Chinatown with the first-ever night market. Enjoy stage performances, food by participating vendors, info booths & arts and crafts for kids.
